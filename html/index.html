System Overview

Reservation Paige is a comprehensive venue reservation management system tailored for State Spaces, a premier coworking and venue provider in Metro City. The platform streamlines the management of customers, complex reservations, diverse venues, multi-story buildings, agents, and amenities. It provides robust support for real-time bookings, renovation tracking, and detailed analytical reporting.
